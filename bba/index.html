BBA

About

Dr. Sumeet Khurana

Head of Department

Faculty of Management Studies

Anything is possible for those with a dream, a plan and the determination to make it happen. BBA is such a course which is designed in such a way so as to bring out the best from the individuals and making them competent to cross all the hurdles of their life. The course is made to focus on the streams like Marketing, Finance, Management, Entrepreneurship, International Business, Resource Management etc. BBA will not only help in acquiring professional skills but will also give an overall understanding on business processes and will help in developing business skills such as communication, leadership and decision making. The institution has highly competent, committed and proficient faculty members who have associated their personal aims and growth processes with that of the institution. We concentrate on providing soft skills with special emphasis on Spoken English, Role plays, Case discussions, Mock interviews, Group discussions etc.

Intake, Affiliation and Duration

  • Intake: 60
  • Duration: 03 Years
  • Approved By: UGC
  • Affiliation: DAVV, Indore.

Vision

To inculcate values which empower students to face challenges and develop vibrant academic environment for the best quality education.

Mission

To nurture the skills which are highly required by the service sector by producing more holistic managers who will practice the management profession with a deeper understanding of global challenges. A rigorous programme focusing on 360-degree development, one to one mentoring, exposure to corporate working, practical oriented teaching.

Program Outcomes

  • Demonstrate the knowledge of management science to solve complex corporate problems using limited resources.
  • Research, identify and analyze management research problems.
  • Identify business opportunities, design and implement innovations in the workplace.
  • Apply ethical principles and make ethical choices.
  • To function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings.
  • Communicate effectively with all the stakeholders.
  • Engage in independent and life-long learning.

Educational Objectives

  • Demonstrate language skills.
  • Demonstrate oral communication skills.
  • Demonstrate knowledge of foundational subjects for management studies.
  • Demonstrate an awareness of ethical understanding.

Extra Certifications & Skill Enhancement Programmes

The increasing competition in various areas like Finance, Information Technology, Management etc, demands Extra Certifications and helps the students to stand ahead of others. At the time of recruitment, companies hire the employees not only on the basis of their degree but a preference is given to those candidates who are Certified Professionals. These Certifications helps students to showcase their competence, commitment for the profession, build expertise in their respective field, and helps them with job advancement. It is a designation earned by a person giving a kind of assurance to the company of their calibre of performing a job in a more professional way.

We at College of Professional Studies, Indore offer various Skill Enhancement and Industry Ready Certification Programmes:

NCFM Certifications (National Stock Exchange Certification in Financial Markets)

A critical element of the financial sector reforms is the development of a pool of human resources having right skills and expertise in each segment of the industry to provide quality intermediation to market participants. NSE Academy Certification in Financial Markets, or NCFM, program is an online testing and certification program that tests the practical knowledge and skills required to operate in the financial markets.

 Modules of NCFM

  1. Financial Market Module
  2. Commercial Banking in India Module
  3. Mutual Funds Beginner’s Module
  4. Securities and Capital Market Basic Modules
  5. Banking Fundamentals Module.
