Campus

The SKITM Campus is set in the backdrop of the serene Ralamandal Hills. All the flora and fauna gives the campus a unique personality like no other. Its a campus which exudes a peaceful environment and makes for a great place to learn and imbibe knowledge. The sprawling campus is spread over 12 acres of land with over 300,000 ft. of infrastructure in place.

Read More

Central Library

At present 24,615 volumes are available in the library. Library is automated and ERP(IFW version 2.0 web version) is the software used for Circulation, OPAC etc. All the library books are bar-coded and books are issued to users by reading the barcode of the document.
