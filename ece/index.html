Electronics & Communication Engineering

About ECE

Dr. Amit Udawat

Head of Department

Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering

The sole purpose of the department is to help students to discover their capabilities. We have consistently maintained a commendable academic record.

Our objective is academic excellence and quality education, to enhance the employable skills of students.

Intake Affiliation and Duration:

  • Program: B.Tech. (Electronics & Communication Engineering)
  • Intake: 60
  • Duration: 04 Years
  • Approved By: AICTE
  • Affiliation: Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal

Vision

  • Department development into a full-fledged centre of learning and research in various fields of Electronics and Communication Engineering keeping in view the latest developments.
  • Student’s development in attaining a solid foundation in Electronics and Communication Engineering fundamentals with an attitude to pursue continuing education.
  • Students functionality professionally and technically in an increasingly international and rapidly changing world due to the advances in technologies and concepts.
  • Students practicing excellent leadership qualities, at levels appropriate to their experience, which addresses issues in a responsive, ethical, and innovative manner.
  • Students performing excellently in careers by being a part of success and growth of an organization with which they are associated.
  • Students contributing to the needs of the society in solving technical problems using Electronics and Communication Engineering principles, tools, and practices.

Mission

To provide education for the students who are able to compete internationally on technical and professional grounds, able to generate creative solutions to the society’s needs, conscious to the universal moral values, adherent to the professional ethical code, and to produce and disseminate knowledge and technologies essential to the local and global needs in the field of electronics and communication engineering.

Programme Outcomes

  • Acquiring adequate knowledge on fundamentals of engineering sciences, applied mathematics and computational aspects and to apply the comprehension gained on laboratory experiments.
  • Possessing good listening, reading, writing and spoken skills on technical English communications.
  • Applying the communicative language skills and professional knowledge to gain job opportunities in leading organizations.
  • Motivated with an interest to undergo higher studies or research in India or foreign countries.
  • Ability to design, construct and test application oriented circuits and systems related analog/digital/mixed signal based electronics, signal processing and telecommunication engineering.
  • Developing skills to carry our experiments listed in the curriculum and to implement individual/team projects.
  • Learning professional, managerial, ethical and environmental based principles to become excellent citizens of our nation.
  • Ability to use computers for programming, simulation, documentation, calculation, slide presentation and other application based needs such as Photoshop, etc.
  • Acquiring required broader knowledge on all other engineering disciplines including engineering graphics and engineering practices.
  • Flexibility to execute team work without any disparity based on gender, interdisciplinary, etc.
  • Presenting technical research papers in the National / International conferences, reputed journals, workshops and symposia.

Educational Objectives

  • To impart knowledge to students on the fundamentals of basic science, mathematics and computer science that would help them to comprehend the design and developmental concepts of Electronics and Communication Engineering.
  • To develop the indispensable communication skills and essential comprehensive abilities in students and provide them with hardware and software skill training that will facilitate them to attain excellent job opportunities in the industries or for their higher studies.
  • To provide practical skills through hands-on experience to the students to relate the knowledge gained on various scientific and technical courses in order to carry out curriculum based laboratory experiments and team projects.
  • To provide exhaustive knowledge and demonstrations related to the core area of Electronics and Communication Engineering, starting from the fundamentals to the level of analysis, synthesis and design of circuits and systems, in addition to the exposure on the latest advancements in the field.
  • To cultivate the students with a broader knowledge of usage of computers, management, ethical and moral principles, environmental issues and other engineering disciplines.
  • To familiarize the students with the concept of state of art, computing techniques and simulations which help them to incorporate new ideas in any field of technology in order to make advancements in the already existing knowledge.
  • To foster an interest for self learning, updating recent developments, innovations in the major area of study by way of plant training and industrial visit and motivating to present in national/international forums for dissemination of knowledge.

Training Available

Embedded Systems and Applications of AVR Microcontroller

Applications of IoT – Students are trained regarding applications of IOT for the society in various domains like industrial, medical, armed forces and many more. Details of supporting technologies like Raspberry-Pi and Aurdino are also shared with the students.

Training on the topic “Evolution of Technologies in Hyper-Connected World”.

A three week training program on Introduction to MATLAB Applications, MATLAB Fundamentals, Simulink Signal Processing Toolbox, Communications Toolbox, Antenna Toolbox, Image Processing Toolbox.

Industrial Automation using PLC and SCADA – Organized and coordinated Industrial Training programs in association with Autosys, Indore.

PCB Designing & Fabrication” is conducted under the aegis of ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education) Students Chapter – Hands on training for designing PCB on Eagle software followed by hardware fabrication and component assembly were covered. After circuit fabrication testing is done by the students to ensure proper working of the circuit.

Electronics Devices and Circuits Lab

In this lab students can study the characteristics of electronic components and measuring instruments.

They can analyze the characteristics PN, Zener diode and LED, design rectifiers with and without filters, analyze transistor characteristics, study frequency response of amplifiers, measure frequency, phase of signals.

 

 

 

 

Digital Circuits Lab

This lab is dealing with digital Circuits and processing of them.

  1. Know building blocks used in digital circuit designing.
  2. To understand the implementation of combinational and sequential circuits using the minimization techniques (K-maps, Quine–McCluskey algorithm)
  3. Be aware of applications of the combinational circuits and sequential circuits in the real world scenarios by implementing different circuits.

 

 

Electronic Instrumentation Lab

Deals with: Measurement of L, R, C and Physical parameter pressure, strain, flow, Temperature, AC/ DC Bridges, Measurement through CRO and Calibration, Water level measurement.

  • Wide range of transducer such as temperature, optical, LVDT ,strain gauge etc
  • Function generators, CROs,
  • Digital Multimeter
  • PLC kit
  • AC /DC Bridge
  • Oscillators

 

 

 

Analog and digital communication Lab

Testing and verification of basic communication techniques such as amplitude modulation, DSB, SSB, FM, PCM, delta, angle modulation and digital modulation schemes, It also incorporate FDM and TDM Techniques.

  • DSB/SSB AM transmitter and Receiver trainer
  • Frequency modulation/Demodulation trainer
  • FM Communication trainer AC /DC Bridge
  • Frequency Division Multiplexing/Demultiplexing
  • TDM Pulse amplitude modulation/ Demodulation trainer
  • TDM Pulse code modulation transmitter/Receiver trainer
  • Sampling and reconstruction trainer
  • Noise Audio Amplifier trainer
  • Delta, Adaptive Delta sigma modulation and Demodulation trainer, and many more.

 

Embedded Lab

Verification and design of digital circuits MUX/DEMUX, Encoder, Counters etc.It deals the programming of 8086 microprocessor and its interfacing with 8255,8253, 8279 and other interfacing ICs.

  • 8086 microprocessor trainer kit with LCD display & ASCII Keyboard
  • Kit for various interfacing ICs with processors 8255 Study Card ,8253 Study Card,8279 study card and many more.

 

 

 

 

Advance Communication and Television Engineering Lab

This lab deals with the mobile communication, optical communication and television systems.

  • CDMA Trainer
  • GSM Trainer
  • Fiber optic trainer using LED Source Frequency Division Multiplexing/Demultiplexing
  • Fiber optic Multiplexer/Demultiplexer trainer using Laser Source TDM Pulse code modulation transmitter/Receiver trainer
  • Fiber optic trainer for PC to PC communication Noise Audio Amplifier trainer
  • Optical power meter
  • Color TV Trainers
